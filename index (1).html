The problem we solve

Most ABA Centers Are Running on Broken Systems

Great clinicians can't deliver great outcomes when the infrastructure around them is missing. Here's what we see in centers every day.

⚠️

No ITP Framework

BCBAs are writing plans from scratch with no center-wide structure — inconsistent quality, missed goals, and no scalability.

⏱️

Goals Not Running at Intervals

Without a 7.5-minute interval system, client goals get skipped, deprioritized, or run inconsistently across staff and shifts.

📅

Client Schedules Aren't Working

Kids aren't following routines because the scheduling system behind those routines was never properly designed or implemented.

🛡️

DHS & EIDBI Exposure

Weak documentation, inconsistent program delivery, and missing systems create real compliance risk for EIDBI-enrolled providers.

ABA Systems Consult comes in and builds the system your center has been missing — a clinical operating infrastructure that every BCBA follows, every client fits into, and every staff member can execute on the floor.

About

Built by a BCBA Who Has Seen What's Missing

ABA Systems Consult was founded after 17 years of working inside schools, homes, and clinics — and repeatedly seeing the same problem: talented BCBAs trying to do great clinical work inside centers that had no real system to support them.

Goals weren't being run at the right intervals. Kids weren't following schedules because those schedules were never properly built. ITPs had no consistent framework. The result was inconsistent outcomes and compliance exposure — not because of bad clinicians, but because of missing infrastructure.

This consulting practice exists to fix that — center by center.

  • Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA)
  • 17+ years across schools, in-home & clinic settings
  • Expertise in ITP frameworks & interval-based scheduling
  • Deep knowledge of EIDBI & DHS compliance requirements
  • Serving Minnesota centers and nationwide remotely
